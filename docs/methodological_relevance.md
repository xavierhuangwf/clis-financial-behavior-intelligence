# Methodological Relevance

This repository demonstrates applied experience in building modular machine learning systems for early signal detection, behavioural modelling, interpretable segmentation, and intervention-oriented analytics in regulated data environments.

The project is situated in financial services, but the underlying engineering and analytical patterns are transferable to broader research contexts that require:
- early identification of emerging risk
- efficient transformation of raw data into actionable signals
- interpretable decision-support pipelines
- scalable analytics under operational constraints

This repository should therefore be viewed as a demonstration of transferable technical capability rather than as a direct domain-specific project in healthcare or critical infrastructure.
